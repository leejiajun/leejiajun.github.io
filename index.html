Jiajun Li (李佳骏)

ABOUT ME

I am a Ph.D. student at the Institute of Computing Technology of Chinese Academy of Sciences under the supervision of Professor Yinhe Han and Professor Xiaowei Li. My research interests primarily focus on the area of Hardware/Software Co-design for reducing power and improving performance with specialized brain-inspired accelerators.
