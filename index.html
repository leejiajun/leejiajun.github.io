ABOUT ME

I am a Ph.D. Student in Institute of Computing Technology of Chinese Academy of Sciences under supervision of Professor Yinhe Han and Professor Xiaowei Li. Before coming to CAS, I obtained my Bachelor degree in 2016 from Department of Information Engineering, China University of Geosciences (Wuhan). My research interests primarily focus on the area of Hardware/Software Co-design for reducing power and improving performance with specialized brain-inspired accelerators.
